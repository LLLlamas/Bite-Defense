🐾 Welcome to Bite Defense!

Defend your Dog HQ from waves of cats. Build, train, and battle.

🏗️ Getting Started

  • Tap 🛒 Store to pick a building. You can only place one at a time.
  • Start with a 💧 Water Well and a 🥛 Milk Farm — these generate your two resources.
  • Water and Milk are interchangeable currency for most buildings (pay with whichever you have).

⚔️ Training Your Dogs

  • Build a ⚔️ Training Camp to recruit Soldiers and Archers.
  • Build a 🛡️ Fort to house them — each troop takes slots equal to its level.
  • Trained dogs stay garrisoned until battle — they only appear on the map in Pre-Battle.

🐶 You Need Dog Troops!

You don't have any dogs trained yet — the cats will tear through your base!

🏗️ Get Ready

  • Open the 🛒 Store and build a ⚔️ Training Camp to recruit Soldiers & Archers.
  • Build a 🛡️ Fort so your trained dogs have somewhere to huddle up.
  • Once trained, dogs stay garrisoned in the Fort until the next battle.

⚔️ When You're Ready

At the start of the wave, spread your troops out toward the direction the cats are coming from — watch the red spawn marker!

⚠️ Pre-Battle: Position Your Troops!

A marker has appeared at the corner where the 🐱 cats will spawn.

  • Tap a dog to select it, then tap a tile to move it there.
  • Tap a Training Camp or Fort to set a rally point — troops will regroup there.
  • Position your soldiers between the spawn corner and your HQ.

💰 What's at Stake

If your dogs fall in battle, the cats will steal your Water and Milk — more on harder difficulties!
